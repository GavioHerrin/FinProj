    Game Sites Creators

This is about our class and the teacher of our class

Teacher: Kristen Haubold

This course is also called Computer Science Principles. Students will gain a broad understanding of the basic principles of computer science. Using MITs Scratch and App Inventor, as well as the industry standard languages of Python, HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, MySQL and others, students plan, design, and create games, apps, programs, and websites. Using Python as a primary tool, students explore and become inspired by career paths that utilize computing, discover tools that foster creativity and collaboration, and use what they have learned to tackle challenges like app development and simulation. This course is endorsed by the College Board, giving students the opportunity to take the AP CSP exam for college credit.
